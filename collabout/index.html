Collabout

Planning dinner or organizing a grocery trip shouldn't require a massive, expensive project management tool.

90% of the time, all you really need is a fast way to keep track of what everyone is doing, and a way to make quick decisions. That's why during a 24-hour Facebook Hackathon, we built this service that makes it simple to create synced, shareable to-do lists and polls.

We think you're going to love it.

Features

So, how does it work? All you have to do is create a page, and then send collaborators a link. Everything people put on the project page is synced live. That means you can:

  • Watch as people check off tasks, live.
  • Add polls to decide on meeting times, locations, and more
  • Access everything from any smartphone.
