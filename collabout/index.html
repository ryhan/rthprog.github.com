Collabout

Planning dinner or organizing a grocery trip shouldn't require a massive, expensive project management tool.

We noticed that 90% of the time, all we really needed was a way to keep track of what everyone was doing, as well as way to make quick decisions. So, we built this service in 24 hours at a Facebook hackathon to make it simple to create synced, shareable to-do lists and polls.

We think you're going to love it.

Features

Just create a page, and send collaborators a link.

Everything you put the project page is synced live.

  • Watch as people check off tasks, live.
  • Add polls to decide on meeting times, locations, and more
  • Access everything from any smartphone.
